Mentorship Program

Connect with current students to share your expertise and help shape the next generation of professionals.

About the Mentorship Program

Our mentorship program connects experienced alumni with current students and recent graduates who are seeking guidance in their academic and professional journeys.

Mentorship relationships provide valuable insights, networking opportunities, and personalized advice that can help mentees navigate their career paths more effectively.

Program Benefits

For Mentors

  • Give back to your alma mater
  • Develop leadership and coaching skills
  • Expand your professional network
  • Gain fresh perspectives from new talent

For Mentees

  • Receive personalized guidance and advice
  • Build valuable industry connections
  • Develop professional skills
  • Gain confidence in career decisions

How It Works

1

Registration

Alumni register as mentors and students register as mentees, both indicating their areas of interest and expertise.

2

Matching

Our system suggests potential matches based on shared interests, goals, and expertise.

3

Connection

Mentors and mentees connect and establish communication channels and expectations.

4

Ongoing Relationship

Regular meetings and check-ins help build a meaningful mentorship relationship.

Ready to Get Involved?

Whether you're looking to share your experience or seeking guidance, our mentorship program has something for you.

Become a Mentor

Share your professional experience and insights with current students and recent graduates. As a mentor, you'll have the opportunity to make a meaningful impact on the next generation of professionals.

What Makes a Good Mentor?

  • Professional experience in your field
  • Willingness to share knowledge and insights
  • Good listening and communication skills
  • Empathy and patience
  • Commitment to regular communication
  • Interest in helping others grow and develop

Being a mentor has been one of the most rewarding experiences of my career. Watching my mentees grow and succeed gives me immense satisfaction, and I've learned so much from them too.

James Wilson

Marketing Director, Class of 2015
